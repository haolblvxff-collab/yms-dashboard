### 1. Semiconductor Manufacturing Handbook, 2nd Edition ⭐ 强烈推荐

**直接访问链接**:
- **AccessEngineering** (需机构订阅): https://www.accessengineeringlibrary.com/content/book/9781259642821
- **Amazon 购买**: https://www.amazon.com/Semiconductor-Manufacturing-Handbook-Second-Edition/dp/1259642828
- **Google Books 预览**: https://www.google.com/books/edition/Semiconductor_Manufacturing_Handbook_Second_Edition/XYZ (搜索书名)

**ISBN**: 978-1259642821  
**主编**: Hwaiyu Geng  
**出版社**: McGraw-Hill Education (2018)  
**估价**: ~$150 USD (纸质版)

**章节目录** (根据第 1 版推测):
1. Introduction to Semiconductor Manufacturing
2. Silicon Wafer Preparation
3. Oxidation
4. Photolithography
5. Etching
6. Thin Film Deposition (PVD, CVD, ALD)
7. Ion Implantation and Diffusion
8. Chemical Mechanical Polishing
9. Metallization
10. Process Control and Yield Management ← **重点章节**
11. Testing and Packaging
12. Cleanroom and Environmental Control

**阅读建议**:
- Phase 1: 第 1-2 章（概述 + 晶圆制备）
- Phase 1: 第 10 章（工艺控制与良率管理）
- Phase 2: 第 3-9 章（各工艺详细）

## 🔍 检索技巧

### Google Scholar 高级搜索

**按时间筛选**:
```
左边栏 → Any time → Since 2020
```

**按引用数排序**:
```
左边栏 → Sort by citations
```

**查找 PDF**:
```
搜索词 + filetype:pdf
例如：CMOS process flow tutorial filetype:pdf
```

**查找综述**:
```
搜索词 + (review OR tutorial OR overview)
例如：yield management semiconductor review
```
